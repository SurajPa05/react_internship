LETS MAKE A TEE
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup full-fat milk
  • 2 teaspoons black tea leaves or granules (e.g., Assam or a strong CTC blend)
  • 2–3 teaspoons sugar (adjust to taste)

For the Masala (Spice Blend):

  • 1 inch fresh ginger, crushed
  • 2–3 green cardamom pods, lightly crushed
  • 3–4 cloves (optional)
  • 1 small cinnamon stick (optional)
